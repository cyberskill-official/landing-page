# Saigon Dusk Mood Board — Rationale

**Status:** v1.0.0, 2026-05-16. Source: FR-DS-001 §3.5.

---

## Cluster A — Saigon Dusk palette (4-6 refs)

Photographic anchors for warm-brown + saffron-gold. Each demonstrates the pairing in real Vietnamese context — something a Saigon eye sees daily. Include: Bát Tràng pottery glaze close-up; sơn mài lacquerware detail (gold leaf inlaid in brown); Vietnamese coffee (cà phê đen — phin drip); Saigon at golden hour (urban texture, NOT a tourist landmark); Buddhist temple light with saffron robes against warm wood.

These refs ratify §3.2's palette as ambient — not invented. Reading we want: *"yes, this pair is everywhere here; it's a geography choice, not a brand one."*

---

## Cluster B — Vietnamese material register (3-4 refs)

Texture references signalling materiality WITHOUT iconography. Handmade paper (giấy dó); woven bamboo / rattan (a chair, not a tourist hat); raw silk in natural dye; optional mid-process lacquer showing visible craft.

These anchor the contemporary-casual register FR-CHAR-003's cultural-note requires. When we reach for a Vietnamese cue, we reach for material + craft — not áo dài + dragons.

---

## Cluster C — Cinematic web (3-4 refs)

Award-winning sites we emulate for **composition and craft only** — palette stays ours. Required references:

- **Igloo Inc. abeto** (Awwwards SOTY 2024) — for the hybrid scroll + 3D pattern. Closest existing reference to our intended UX.
- **Lusion v3** (Awwwards SOTY 2023) — for baked-asset craft and studio-website-as-portfolio confidence.
- **One of:** Immersive Garden V4 / 14islands V4 / Active Theory The Field — for gltf-Transform automation OR progressive-enhancement WebGL OR volumetric-particle techniques.

Every cluster-C reference carries the annotation: *"composition + craft only; palette is ours."* Without this, mood-board readers conflate the cool-toned aesthetics of those sites with our intended warm-only direction.

---

## Cluster D — Mascots that work in B2B (2-4 refs)

Mascot references that maintain professional warmth without becoming twee:

- **Messenger SOTY 2025** character — proof that "cute character with a job" can win SOTY.
- **One of:** Mailchimp Freddie / Linear circles / Notion icons — minimal, professional, warm. Anti-references to whatever is the opposite (e.g. enterprise-mascot-as-cartoon-businessman).

Cluster D guards Lumi against drift toward cartoon over the 18-week build.

---

## Counter-examples ("we are NOT this")

Two explicit ANTI-references, framed with red border and AVOID label:

1. **Tourist-Vietnamese composition:** stock photo of an áo dài model in front of a temple in golden hour. Reasoning: leads with heritage / exoticism / tourism, none of which is the brand register.
2. **Cool-toned tech-shop default:** cyan-on-navy SaaS landing page. Reasoning: indistinguishable from FPT pricing tier; defeats the entire Saigon Dusk thesis.

These counter-examples are AS IMPORTANT as the positive cluster — they pre-empt the most common drift directions.

---

## What's deliberately absent

- No cool-tone primaries (cyan / blue / magenta as dominant) — master plan §3.2 mandate.
- No dragons, phoenixes, kirin, ceremonial áo dài, lotus motifs — FR-CHAR-003 + this mood board agree.
- No cost-led framing ("affordable", "outsourcing", "low rates") — master plan §1.4 strategy synthesis: "do not lead with cost."
