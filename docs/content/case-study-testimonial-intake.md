# Case study and testimonial intake

Send me the items below and I will wire them in, replacing the current honest
placeholders. Everything published must be cleared by the client - when in
doubt, anonymize (an agreed label like "a Series A logistics operator" works and
still converts).

## Per case study (we have three slots: operations platform, member mobile app, commerce portal)

Send, for each:

1. Client or company name, or an agreed anonymized label.
2. Confirmation it is cleared to publish on the public site.
3. One-line result, concrete and honest (e.g. "cut order-processing time" - keep
   a number only if it is real and cleared).
4. The challenge, in two or three sentences (what was hard before).
5. What we did, in two or three sentences.
6. The outcome, in two or three sentences (qualitative is fine; no invented
   percentages).
7. Three to five tags (e.g. Next.js, offline-first, Core Web Vitals).
8. Optional: a logo or screenshot you have the right to use.

Both English and Vietnamese, if you have them; otherwise send English and flag
the Vietnamese for the native pass.

## Per testimonial

1. The quote, verbatim and approved.
2. Attribution: name, role, company (or an agreed anonymized form).
3. Confirmation it is cleared to publish.
4. Optional: a headshot or company logo you have rights to.

## What I will not do

I will not invent client names, quotes, outcomes, or numbers. The page stays on
honest placeholders until you send cleared material - that protects the brand and
your credibility with the buyers this site is meant to win.

## Format

Plainest is fine: a short message, a doc, or a spreadsheet row per item. I will
format, translate-pending-review, and place them, then show you a preview before
it goes live.
