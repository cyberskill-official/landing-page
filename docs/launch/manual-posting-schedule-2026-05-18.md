## Founder Reflection

Channel: LinkedIn founder page, launch + 2 days.

```text
Small design note from the new CyberSkill site:

Lumi wears a casual non la, not as a tourism symbol or costume, but as an everyday Vietnamese marker. We wanted the site to feel like it came from here without turning culture into decoration.

That same idea shaped the engineering choices. The cinematic version is there for visitors who can enjoy it. The /lite route, captions, keyboard paths, and no-client-tracker analytics are there because the story still needs to work when motion, hardware, or privacy expectations differ.

The best software work does both: it carries a point of view, and it respects the user in front of it.
```
